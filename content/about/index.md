## About Me

Hi, I'm Yu-Jie (Marcos).

I currently focus on SRE and DevOps, with a particular interest in how systems maintain reliability targets under high traffic and real-time demands, and whether they have good observability to quickly identify and recover from inevitable failures.

I've been deeply involved in distributed systems and cloud architecture, often making trade-offs between reliability, performance, cost, and delivery speed. Rather than pursuing idealized designs, I care more about: whether risks are clearly understood, whether failures are predictable, and whether systems have sufficient resilience.

This blog records these reflections, and it's not just about technology. You'll find:

**TECH**: Practical experiences and design trade-offs in SRE / DevOps, cloud, and distributed systems
